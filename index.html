About ByteSynergy


ByteSynergy, is a 52 hour long hackathon conducted by the students of IIIT Bangalore as a part of Synergy - The Techfest of IIITB. It is a chance to put your creative minds against some of the best student developers in our country.


Hack away and bring your ideas to fruition, using any techstack, language, or open source codebase! Make apps that will not only contribute to the world of open source development, but also help people improve their quality of life.

All students currently enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate degree program are welcomed to participate in the hackathon.

Although prior experience in coding will be an advantage, the teams also require a great idea and amazing presentation skills to win. If you arent experienced, this hackathon will be a great place to begin!

The maximum team size allowed is four, but smaller teams are also welcome.

Participating in this hackathon is absolutely free! Just join in and have fun!

You can participate alone! If you want to find any teammates, we will try our best to match you with someone!
